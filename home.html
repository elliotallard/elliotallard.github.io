HACKeD Hackathon: October 28th, 2017

We hosted a fantastic hackathon this October. We had over 35 people attend, and 10 beautiful projects. Google and Schell Games were able to support the event the event, and the projects were judged by Dr. Chinmay Kulkarni, Michael Madaio, and Judith Brooks. Check out the Facebook page!

Potential projects

The club went to the Schell Games Open House. It was a showcase of video games the company had developed, and we were lucky enough to try some out. Check out the photos and videos of the trip on Facebook!
