FAQ

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is an event where people from all backgrounds can come together to work on a project that inspires them and possibly could change the world.

Who can attend?

Any current CMU student is welcomed to come to this event!

Where is it located?

NOTE ADD SPECIFIC LOCATION Carnegie Mellon University, Pittsbury, PA 15213

What should I bring?

All you need is a laptop and some passion to try out something new. We will provide everything else (including lunch, dinner, and swag)!

What if I dont have a team or an idea?

No problem! We'll help atendees form teams of up to 4 people and menors will be around to provide guidance and support.

What can I build?

Anything you want that is related to education and technology. It can be a game, a toll for classroons, a homework helper, or anything else you think of!
