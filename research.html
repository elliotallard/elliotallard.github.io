We aim to make the CMU community more aware of the educational technology resources, programs, and research already on campus, and also to build stronger connections between these existing components of our campus.


Below is a short listing of things at CMU that are related to educational technology in some way.

Academic Programs


Undergraduate
IDEATE Learning Media - Interdisciplinary undergraduate minor and concentration.

Graduate
METALS – Masters in Educational Technology and Applied Learning Science, a brand new program

PIER – Program in Interdisciplinary Education Research, a doctoral training program

ETC - Entertainment Technology Center, which not only works to entertain but to educate

Research

LearnLab - CMU-affiliated science of learning research center, funded by the NSF

HCI Research - research in the Human-Computer Interaction Institute about learning science and educational technology

LTI Research - research within the Language Technologies Institute on education

The Children's School - a preschool/kindergarten program on CMU's campus from which much early education research springs from

Carnegie Mellon Create Lab - robotics innovation lab with educational-related projects

Resources and Initiatives

Simon Initiative - Carnegie Mellon's campaign towards educational technology innovation

Tech Bridge World - technology projects benefitting underserved communities

Leonard Gelfand Center - tutoring service opportunities at local Pittsburgh schools

DataShop - the world's largest open database of learning interaction data
